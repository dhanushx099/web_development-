WASEL DISTRIBUTORS TM

Wasel Distribution, founded in 2025, is a specialized distribution company dedicated to the efficient and high-quality delivery of grape pulp across regional and international markets. Established with a vision to bridge the gap between grape producers and manufacturers in the food and beverage industry, Wasel Distribution has quickly positioned itself as a reliable partner in the supply chain. The company emphasizes sustainable sourcing, rigorous quality control, and timely logistics to ensure that its grape pulp products meet the highest standards. With a focus on innovation and customer satisfaction, Wasel Distribution is committed to supporting the growth of industries that rely on natural, fruit-based ingredients.
